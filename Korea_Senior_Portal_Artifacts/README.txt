Intent
A comprehensive portal for all things senior in Korea: care, medical history, medicine tracking, subsidy eligibility overlay, nursing-hospital / medical-institution linkage, caregiver matching, local-government info, housing / silver town, senior opportunities, and observability.

Notes
- Everyone can use the portal without meeting subsidy eligibility.
- Eligibility matters only when the user wants subsidized programs.
- Consent-based data sharing with medical and government institutions is central.
